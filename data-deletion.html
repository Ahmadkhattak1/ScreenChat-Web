Data Deletion

These are the current ways to delete saved ScreenChat data.

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Option 1: Delete saved conversation history inside the extension

ScreenChat includes built-in history controls inside the extension UI.

  • Open ScreenChat and go to the History view.
  • Delete an individual saved conversation from the list.
  • Use Clear all conversations to remove all saved conversation history for your ScreenChat account.

Option 2: Request deletion of stored ScreenChat account data by email

If you want ScreenChat account data removed beyond in-app history controls, email ahdfactz@gmail.com.

Please include:

  • The Google account email address you used with ScreenChat.
  • The subject line ScreenChat data deletion request.

What gets deleted

  • Saved ScreenChat conversation history associated with your ScreenChat account.
  • Stored ScreenChat profile information associated with your account.

What does not get deleted

  • Your Google account itself is not deleted.
  • Signing out stops future use on that browser, but it does not delete previously stored ScreenChat data unless you clear history or request deletion.

Screenshots

If you use Attach Screen, the screenshot is sent with that request to help generate a reply. It is not saved as part of ScreenChat conversation history.

Contact

For deletion requests or follow-up questions, contact ahdfactz@gmail.com.

Fastest option

Use the in-app history controls first

If you only want saved chats removed, the built-in History view is the fastest path because it lets you delete one conversation or clear all saved conversations directly.

Need account data removed?

Email ahdfactz@gmail.com using the same Google account address you used with ScreenChat.
